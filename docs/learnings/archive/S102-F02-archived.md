# S102-F02 — POT below composite is valid for post-peak NBA vets (ARCHIVED)

**Status:** Superseded — structurally subsumed by S119 NBA-PROJECTION-OUTPUT-BLOCK.md design (2026-05-03).

**Archive note:** This learning's pattern ("POT may legitimately land below composite for post-peak vets meeting ≥2/3 conditions") was a workaround for the prospect-arc PROJECTION-OUTPUT-BLOCK.md Block 2 no-slot path, which defaulted POT above composite via a composite-to-POT band table. The S119 NBA POB replaces that machinery entirely with anchor-based projection — and the new POB's Step 3.1 ("POT cannot fall below the player's best observed season composite") plus Validation Check #2 (POT ≤ demonstrated peak + career-stage allowance; post-peak/late "should not exceed at all") encode post-peak handling structurally rather than via a calibration patch on top of an inflation-prone default.

Under the new NBA POB, every one of this learning's 6 firings (Sabonis S102 −0.62; Vučević S104 −0.33; Embiid S110 −0.80; Giannis S116 −0.30; KD S119 −0.56; Curry S123 −0.70) would be impossible — POT cannot drop below demonstrated peak, which for these post-peak-recent-peak cases equals current composite. The pattern's diagnostic value (the OLD POB inflated post-peak POT via the no-slot band default) is preserved here for provenance. Future projection-doc changes that reintroduce a composite-to-POT band default should re-load this entry and re-evaluate.

### S102-F02 — POT below composite is valid for post-peak NBA vets
**Date:** 2026-04-24 (original); extended 2026-04-24 (S104 — second application + first negative-control); extended 2026-04-24 (S105 — second negative-control confirms age-bracket discrimination)
**Severity:** Medium
**Context:** Sabonis Skill 5 (S102) surfaced a projection pattern that conflicts with the surface reading of PROJECTION-OUTPUT-BLOCK.md's no-slot path table. The table prescribes "Composite 8.0–10.0 → POT 9.00–9.99" — which defaults POT above composite. But for Sabonis (age 29, 2024-25 career-best at composite 8.22, season-ending meniscus injury Feb 2026, Kings in rebuild with 3-year playoff dormancy, R13 historical-only + strong shrink), applying modifier stack from range midpoint (9.50) produced POT 7.60 — **below composite 8.22 by 0.62**. This is correct for a post-peak vet whose ceiling going forward is bounded by age, trajectory, team context, and accumulated injury/shrink signals. Forcing POT into the 9.00-9.99 band would have inflated projection beyond defensible forward-ceiling.

**S104 extension — Vučević second application:** Vučević (age 35, composite 7.78, 2024-25 career-year followed by 2025-26 role shift post-trade + ring-finger surgery, R13 neutral) produced POT 7.45 — **below composite 7.78 by 0.33**. Met 2/3 conditions (flat/declining trajectory + documented physical ceiling converged at #17/#21/#22 all at 4.5). Pattern confirmed on second post-peak vet case.

**S104 contrast-case — Naz Reid first negative control:** Naz Reid (age 26, composite 7.75, ascending career arc with minor shoulder late-season, R13 rise) produced POT 8.55 — **above composite 7.75 by 0.80**. Met 0-1/3 conditions (recent plateau partial; no significant injury; R13 shows rise not shrink). Standard no-slot path applied; pattern does NOT fire. Age-bracket discrimination (<28 vs 28+) working as intended.

**S105 extension — Şengün second negative control:** Şengün (age 23, composite 8.45, ascending career arc with career-year 2025-26 + 2x All-Star + EuroBasket near-MVP, R13 moderate shrink absorbed via Stage 1 only) produced POT 9.10 — **above composite 8.45 by 0.65**. Met 0/3 post-peak conditions (clearly ascending trajectory; no significant injury; R13 Stage 1 shrink does not constitute the multi-condition test alone). Standard no-slot path applied with ascending-trajectory modifier +0.40 dominating; pattern does NOT fire. Age-bracket discrimination held cleanly across two age points (Reid 26, Şengün 23) on the ascending side.

**S106 extension — Duren third negative control:** Duren (age 22, composite 8.40, 4th-year leap with 1st All-Star + anchoring league top-3 defense, R13 below sample minimum — narrative-only) produced POT 8.90 — **above composite 8.40 by 0.50**. Met 0/3 post-peak conditions (ascending multi-dimensional trajectory; no significant injury; R13 below sample so neither Stage fires — no shrink absorption). Standard no-slot path applied with ascending +0.40 + elite physical ceiling +0.40 − documented physical ceiling −0.40 stack producing net +0.40. Pattern does NOT fire. Age-bracket discrimination held cleanly across three age points (Duren 22, Şengün 23, Reid 26) on the ascending side. Empirical confirmation: pattern firing 2/2 on post-peak vets and not firing 3/3 on ascending vets — discrimination at age-bracket / multi-condition gate empirically clean.

**S107 extension — Turner age-boundary negative control:** Turner (age 29, composite 7.88, plateaued/mild-decline trajectory after 2024-25 career-best 3PT shooting, R13 moderate rise not shrink, no significant active injury) produced POT 8.00 — **above composite 7.88 by 0.12**. Met 1/3 post-peak conditions (flat trajectory: yes; significant injury: no — 71/72 GP last two seasons, all historical injuries resolved; R13 shrink absorption: no — R13 is moderate rise). Standard no-slot path applied with flat trajectory −0.45 + documented physical ceiling −0.35 + R13 moderate rise +0.20 producing net −0.60 from band midpoint 8.60 → POT 8.00. Pattern does NOT fire. **First negative control at the actual age boundary (29) — the rule's "typically age 28+" language refers to the typical age at which the multi-condition gate fires, not an age-alone trigger.** Validates that age 28+ alone is necessary but not sufficient: a 29-year-old with flat-trajectory but no injury and R13 rise (1/3 conditions) stays on the standard path. Pattern firing 2/2 on post-peak vets meeting ≥2/3 conditions and not firing 4/4 on vets meeting ≤1/3 conditions across age range 22–29.

**S108 extension — Tatum injured-vet negative control at age boundary:** Tatum (age 28, composite 8.88, pre-injury ascending arc 4× All-NBA First Team UNANIMOUS-2025 + 2024 Champion, currently in active proof-of-return from May 2025 right Achilles tear with 16 GP regular season + 3+ playoff games, R13 neutral career playoff TS% delta −1.6) produced POT 9.40 — **above composite 8.88 by 0.52**. Met 1/3 post-peak conditions (significant injury history: YES — Achilles tear is major; flat/declining trajectory: NO — pre-injury ascending was clearly real, current is recovery ramp not decline; R13 shrink absorption: NO — neutral band). Standard no-slot path applied with elite competitive character +0.40 (4× All-NBA unanimous + 2024 Champion + Twyman-Stokes finalist + historic-pace recovery) − significant injury history −0.50 (Achilles, offset by proof-of-return) producing net −0.10 from band midpoint 9.50 → POT 9.40. Pattern does NOT fire. **Second age-boundary case (28) confirms the multi-condition gate works correctly at the boundary itself.** Distinct from Turner case in modifier composition: Turner had flat-trajectory + physical-ceiling stack producing meaningfully-below-band-midpoint POT; Tatum has injury offset by elite-character producing POT close to band-midpoint. Both age-boundary cases stay on standard path because the multi-condition gate requires ≥2/3, not age alone. Pattern firing 2/2 on post-peak vets meeting ≥2/3 conditions and not firing 5/5 on vets meeting ≤1/3 conditions across age range 22–29.

**Learning:** For State 4 NBA-vet evaluations where composite reflects recent-peak production but trajectory is clearly post-peak (flat/declining trajectory + documented injury + R13 shrink absorption — need ≥2 of these conditions), POT may legitimately land below composite. Do NOT force POT into the no-slot table's band midpoint. The no-slot Composite→POT table implicitly assumes ascending trajectory typical of prospect evaluations — for post-peak vets, the modifier stack (flat trajectory, injury history, physical ceiling, R13 Stage 1 shrink) should pull POT to realistic forward ceiling even if that lands below current composite. Document the POT-below-composite outcome explicitly in the projection walkthrough with rationale. **Negative-control pattern (S104 Reid + S105 Şengün):** R13 Stage 1 shrink alone does not flip the pattern — it requires ≥2 of the post-peak conditions; an ascending-trajectory player with only R13 shrink stays on the standard ascending path with POT > composite. **Injury-vet sub-pattern (S108 Tatum):** A major injury alone (1/3 conditions) is also not sufficient to flip the pattern — when paired with elite character / pre-injury ascending arc / active proof-of-return, the standard path holds and POT lands above composite.

**Applies to:** Skill 5 Block 2 POT calculation for State 4 NBA-vet evaluations, specifically post-peak veterans (typically age 28+) meeting the multi-condition test above. Does not apply to prospects (States 1-3) or to NBA vets still on ascending trajectory (Mobley, Şengün-type profiles — Naz Reid S104 + Şengün S105 + Duren S106 confirmed as negative-control contrasts), or to injured-vets in active proof-of-return where pre-injury trajectory was ascending (Tatum S108).
