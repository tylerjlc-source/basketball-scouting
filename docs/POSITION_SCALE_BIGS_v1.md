# POSITION-SPECIFIC SUB-DOMAIN SCALE — BIGS — v1 DRAFT

**Peer group:** Bigs (6 archetypes: All-Around Big, Switchable Big, Rim Protector, Stretch Big, Versatile Scoring Big, Energy Big)
**Peer group rule:** Determined by archetype positional group, not listed position. Any player assigned a Big archetype is scored against this peer group.
**Universal sub-domains:** #12 (Decision-making), #24 (Shot selection), #25 (Effort/motor), #26 (Competitive character) — scored against full NBA population regardless of archetype group. Tables in POSITION_SCALE_UNIVERSAL.md.

**How to use this scale:** Each row defines a band of 5 valid scores (e.g., 8.5–8.9). The descriptor anchors the median of the band (e.g., 8.7). Score at the band median when a player meets the descriptor exactly. Score at the band floor when the player just meets the bar; dial toward the band ceiling when the player exceeds the descriptor and approaches the next-tier descriptor. High/Medium confidence sub-domains (per R4) use full .1 resolution within the band; Low/Very-Low confidence sub-domains land at the band floor only (.0 or .5).

**Note:** Bigs finish at the highest baseline of any peer group — length, strength, vertical leverage, and proximity to the basket combine to produce the league's highest rim-finishing rates. Rim Protector / Switchable Big cluster middle (functional but not scoring-identity).
